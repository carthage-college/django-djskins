    About Carthage

    Carthage's beautiful campus on the shore of Lake Michigan in Kenosha, Wis., is home to 2,500 full-time undergraduate students. Carthage combines an environment of reflection and self-discovery with a culture of high expectation, so that students uncover and ignite their full potential.
